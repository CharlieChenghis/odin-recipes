Piña Colada

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 2 oz coconut cream
  • 2 oz pineapple juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Pineapple slice and maraschino cherry for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend the Ingredients:

    In a blender, combine the white rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice, and a handful of ice cubes. Blend until smooth.

  2. Serve:

    Pour the mixture into a chilled glass (typically a hurricane glass). If the mixture is too thick, you can adjust the consistency by adding a little more pineapple juice or rum.

  3. Garnish:

    Garnish with a slice of pineapple and a maraschino cherry on the rim of the glass.

  4. Enjoy:

    Serve immediately with a straw.
