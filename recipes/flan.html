Flan Recipe

Ingredients

For the Caramel:

  • 1 cup of granulated sugar

For the Flan:

  • 1 can (12 oz) of evaporated milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) of sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).

  2. Make the Caramel:

    In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the sugar until it's liquefied and turns a golden brown color. Be careful not to burn it. Quickly pour the melted sugar into a round baking dish, tilting the dish to evenly coat the bottom.
